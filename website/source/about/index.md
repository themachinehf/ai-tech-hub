## Our Mission

AI Tech Hub is dedicated to making AI and technology education accessible to everyone. We believe in:

- **Accessibility**: Free, high-quality content for learners worldwide
- **Practicality**: Real-world examples and hands-on projects
- **Curiosity**: Fostering a love for learning and experimentation

## What We Do

We create:

1. **In-depth tutorials** - Step-by-step guides for all skill levels
2. **Code examples** - Working code you can use in your projects
3. **Tool reviews** - Honest evaluations of AI tools and platforms
4. **Industry insights** - Trends and analysis from the tech world

## Tech Stack

Our content covers:

- Python, JavaScript, and other languages
- TensorFlow, PyTorch, and JAX
- FastAPI, Flask, and Node.js
- AWS, Vercel, and deployment platforms
